Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how 689plus-sai ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our application, which connects to the Pinterest API to manage Pinterest business account content.

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the following data through the Pinterest API, solely for the purpose of automating content management on our own Pinterest business account:

  • Pinterest account profile information (account name, account ID)
  • Pinterest boards (read and create)
  • Pinterest pins (read and create)
  • Pinterest analytics data (impressions, saves, clicks)

2. How We Use This Information

All data accessed through the Pinterest API is used exclusively to:

  • Create and manage boards on our own Pinterest business account
  • Publish video pins to our own Pinterest business account
  • Retrieve analytics data from our own account for internal reporting

We do not access, collect, store, or process any data belonging to third-party Pinterest users. This application is operated solely for internal business automation purposes by AntiGravity.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens obtained through Pinterest OAuth are stored locally on our own secured systems and are never shared with third parties. We do not maintain any external database of user data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties. Data accessed via the Pinterest API is used solely for the internal automation described in this policy.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses the Pinterest API. Your use of Pinterest is governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect access credentials and any data retrieved from the Pinterest API. Access is restricted to authorized operators of AntiGravity only.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
